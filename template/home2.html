Lift Capacity Management

Lifts are used for mobility of the persons from one floor to another floor. Lifts are used mainly in buildings and malls. But there is a constraint regarding the number of persons residing in the lift at anycurrent point of time should need to be with in the capacity of the lift. Once the number of persons exceed the capacity the lift cannot move. In this fast moving life it is always a tedious process for the persons to count and check whether with in the capacity or not. So our Web application automates the process of managing the number of persons in the lift.
